# Culinary Canvas

Welcome to **Culinary Canvas**, a vibrant social recipe-sharing application. This platform allows food enthusiasts to discover, create, and share their culinary creations with a passionate community.

## ‚ú® Features

-   **Community Feed:** Explore a constantly updating feed of recipes from other users. Sort by newest or top-rated.
-   **Create & Share:** Easily create and publish your own recipes using a rich form.
-   **Personal Cookbook:** Save your favorite recipes to your personal cookbook for quick access.
-   **User Profiles:** Showcase your creations, view your favorite recipes, and see your community stats.
-   **Ratings & Comments:** Engage with the community by rating and commenting on recipes.
